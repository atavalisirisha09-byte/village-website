Administration of Wanaparthy District

Introduction

Wanaparthy district administration is responsible for maintaining law and order, implementing government schemes, and ensuring the development of the district. Various government departments work together to provide services to the people.

District Administration

The District Collector is the head of the district administration and is responsible for revenue, development programs, and public welfare. The Superintendent of Police ensures law and order and public safety in the district.

Mandal Administration

Each mandal in Wanaparthy district is managed by a Tahsildar and Mandal Development Officer. They handle land records, revenue collection, and implementation of government schemes.

Village Administration

The village administration is managed by the Gram Panchayat, led by the Sarpanch. The Panchayat is responsible for village development, sanitation, water supply, roads, and welfare programs.

Government Services

The administration provides various services such as MeeSeva services, issuing certificates, land records management, welfare schemes, and public grievance support.
